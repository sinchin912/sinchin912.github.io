A Big Experiment

July 26, 2021
Analysis

As per Hindu mythology, the time span of known life is divided into 4 parts.

  • Satya Yug : 1,728,000 years
  • Treta Yug : 1,296,000 years
  • Dvapar Yug : 864,000 years
  • Kali Yug : 432,000 years

We are currently in Kali Yug (Kalyug) phase which started approx 5000 years ago (3102 BCE). Here we will be discussing that if this is a part of big experiment. As a software developer, for a developed project, we can have support guidelines as :

  1. There will be 4 phases of the project support
  2. Phase 1 will be of 4 months, any issues would need immmediate fix from developer.
  3. Phase 2 will be of 3 months, issues would need a fix here as per SLA.
  4. Phase 3 will be of 2 months, here the developer had to work with user in isuue identification as system would be more stable than the previous phase. Also to create guidelines for the next phase.
  5. Phase 4 will be of 1 month, since we would be bringing a new system thereafter, thus it would be scratched by developer once the period is over.

If we rephrase certain scale ratio and terminology in above e.g. month=1200 divine years or 1200x360 human years, developer = God, issue = evil being, user = human, we could relate the avatars that our big scientist has taken on this earth. Also Hindu mythology says that this yuga cycle has already taken place many times in the past and will be there in coming future as well.

We know that dinosaurs existed on this earth until 65 million years ago. We also know that most of the dinosaurs were cannibals. Was the asteroid a reset button at the end of Kalyug as the experiment was not going the well as its supposed too i.e. it expected a more human like figure instead of a tailed, heavy, short handed being?
