Decay Constant

August 9, 2021
Analysis

In our previous post, we have talked about how yugas are definitly possible and also how we are practicing similarly in the present world. In this post we will talk more on human characteristics as described for each yuga.

Below is the table that gives average characteristics in each yuga.

Yuga Human height Human Lifespan Human Purity
Satya Yuga 33 ft 1,00,000 years 100%
Treta Yuga 22 ft 10,000 years 75%
Dwapar Yuga 12 ft 1,000 years 50%
Kali Yuga 6 ft 100 years 25%

This means that we are getting shorter, weaker and impure with every moment passed. The population is increasing exponentially in every yuga, which is true as resources (i.e. Earth) available are almost the same.

In Satya Yuga, people don't do farming as natural fruits\vegetables were in abundance. People had no sorrows and no level of cheating was possible.

On the contrary, by the end of Kalyug, any natural resources would come to extinction. People will have just sorrows and it will be easy to kill each other.
