A Bread Mold

July 20, 2021
Analysis

"Do you know we live on round earth, which has a solid crust and a soft core. There is a cover around the earth that helps to keep it warm and sustain life. Else the space is so dark and cold and it is impossible to live there. This universe is so vast that we don't know its actual spread and what could be beyond it." These are the words of an intelligent mold on bun wrapped with plastic and kept in refrigerator.

We as a human have created all kinds of unique, powerful inventions that we expect would help in our existence. From ISS to engineered viruses, C-RAM to super-computer and we feel proud of it. But do our inventions make us capable to secure our existence. If you think we are capable enough looking at present world condition then please watch this video before you rethink.

For us a kilometer can be covered in 5 min walk, but for a bread mold it is a distance that can't be covered in life span. For us 365 days is a time we become just a year older, but for a mold it could be time no-one can ever record. Similarly there can be someone whose kilometer is equal to our light year, and a day in life could be yuga for us. And if all that is possible then our technology and inventions, the most dangerous weapons we created and feel proud of are a waste. In other words, these inventions are only to compete with each other on this earth and stand a no chance with respect to life-form unknown.
